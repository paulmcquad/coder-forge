Start your own Forge

How to start your own Forge

Coder forge plans to expand and have more meetups, more speaker events throughout Dublin and eventually throughout Ireland.

You don't have to be a technical person to start a Forge, however, it is good if you can find a technical person to help you.

In that case, the Forge organisers then, would consist of a founder and a technical facilitator

The process of starting a new Forge

  1. Find a venue. The two main guidelines for finding a venue are: somewhere free and with free acess to wifi. This is important so that you can remain free to members.

  2. Advertise the Forge. Through Meetup, Facebook, the Forge's own website, etc.

    The Forge's own website could be the Forge's first project.

    Susequent possible projects can be found on the resource page of this website.
